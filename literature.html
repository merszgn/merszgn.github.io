Literature & Writing

The existence of little things and details keeps me going most days. My motivation fluctuates often and I constantly find myself in a state of melodramatic despair. I have found that writing has been a helpful coping mechanism for me during these times.

My writing is typically hermeneutic literature that relies heavily upon reader interpretation. My works can have several meanings depending on your own perspective and personal beliefs. I believe this is one of the stronger elements in my writing: the ability to relate to several audiences through one scripture.
