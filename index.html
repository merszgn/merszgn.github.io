Who am I?
My name is Meryem and I'm a Turkish-Muslim university student residing in the Bay Area of California. I graduated high school in two years and am majoring in biology now at the age of sixteen. I am interested in going to medical school to get an M.D. in anesthesiology. "Why anesthesiology?"

I've always had an interest in web development and design. Since 2020, I have been on the internet creating websites and pursuing this hobby publicly. I started my first website on Neocities. I have had multiple anonymous websites on the internet since starting this journey. Now, I've become interested in dedicating a webpage entirely to myself and my thoughts.

This is my own personal Earth. It is my planet that I must take care of and grow whilst maintaining the balance of. My biggest goal with this website is to show others that having their own "Earth" is the most important part of life. It doesn't have to be a website, necessarily, but an extension of your mind that allows you to express yourself freely; something that brings you peace and joy.

What is a Self-Evaluation Journal?
How do you find your peace of mind in the constant rumble of life, work, school, and personality? It's important to have an outlet for all of the struggles, happiness, and anger you may be feeling during these times. A self-evaluation journal is what I like to call my little notebook of questions! If there's a personal question you'd like to answer in the self-eval journal, take a go at it yourself! Write down your thoughts on a paper, a notes app, or even just talk to yourself about it in your head.

A big part of the self-evaluation journal is to be completely honest with yourself. Don't sugarcoat situations to make yourself feel better. It's all about clarity and truth. After all, what's the point of self-evaluation if there's nothing left to evaluate?

The reason I started my own self-evulation journal was to get to know myself better and get to the root of my deepest troubles and regrets. It has helped me grow and become a better version of myself ever since.

A better version of ourselves to create a better version of our Earth.
