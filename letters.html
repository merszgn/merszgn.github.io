Letters To Others

Welcome to my post office! I write letters to people in my life here. They range from being for loved ones that I've known for several years to people I met the day of writing their letter. The most recent letter will appear in the top left. The letters don't have real names on them, but instead the word I associate with each person.
You'll only know it's for you if it feels right.
